1. **HRA (40% of Basic)**
   `=Basic * 40%`

2. **Travel Allowance**
   `=IF(Grade="C",3500,5000)`

3. **Gross Salary**
   `=Basic + HRA + Travel Allowance`

4. **Salary Per Day**
   `=Gross Salary / 30`

5. **Days Worked**
   `=30 - Days Absent`

6. **Net Salary**
   `=Salary Per Day * Days Worked`
